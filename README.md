# Movie-Sales-Analysis

# Objective
* Clean and prepare the IMDb movie dataset for analysis.
* Build interactive dashboards using Power BI.
* Analyze trends in movie release counts, revenue, ratings, and votes.
* Identify top-performing movies and genres.
* To highlight top-performing movies based on votes and popularity, supporting strategic business actions.

# Problem Statement
* The company lacks insights into how movie production and audience preferences have evolved over time.
* Decision-makers need clarity on which genres, years, and directors contribute the most to revenue.
* There is currently no structured understanding of how ratings and votes influence movie performance.
* Without these insights, predicting future trends or investing strategically becomes challenging.

# Conclusion
* Movie production has fluctuated over the years, but specific years stand out as major release peaks, reflecting industry growth cycles.
* IMDb ratings show a shifting trend, indicating changes in storytelling quality and audience expectations.
* Certain genres consistently dominate production and engagement, offering clear direction for future investments.
* Overall, the analysis provides a structured foundation for making informed business decisions in the movie industry.
